What are obstetric services?甚麼是產科服務？

Obstetrics is the medical specialty that cares for women before, during and after pregnancy. Amber Health offers a full range of obstetric services — from the first antenatal visit, through labour and delivery, to postnatal recovery and lactation — delivered by HK-accredited specialists. 產科是專門照顧女性孕前、孕期及產後的醫學專科。Amber Health 提供全面的產科服務 — 由首次產前檢查、分娩、到產後恢復及哺乳支援 — 全由香港認可專科醫生主理。

Who is obstetric care for?產科服務適合誰？

Any woman planning to conceive, currently pregnant, or recently delivered. Care begins ideally at pre-conception and continues through the 40-week journey and six weeks postpartum. Women with a prior caesarean, miscarriage, gestational diabetes, advanced maternal age (≥35), or multiple pregnancy are considered higher-risk and benefit from specialist-led pathways. 任何計劃懷孕、正在懷孕或剛分娩的女性均適用。理想情況下，照護應由孕前開始，延續整個四十週孕程及產後六週。曾接受剖腹產、流產、妊娠糖尿病、高齡產婦（≥35 歲）或多胞胎孕婦屬高風險，更適合由專科主導。

When should I book my first antenatal visit?第一次產前檢查應何時預約？

Between 6 and 10 weeks of pregnancy, as soon as a home test is positive. The first visit confirms viability by ultrasound, establishes the estimated due date, reviews medical and family history, and plans the schedule of screenings (including nuchal translucency at 11–13⁺⁶ weeks and the anomaly scan at 20–22 weeks). 懷孕 6 至 10 週，家用驗孕棒呈陽性後即可預約。首次檢查會以超聲波確認胚胎活動、估算預產期、回顧個人及家族病史，並安排後續篩查（包括 11–13⁺⁶ 週頸項透明帶及 20–22 週結構篩查）。

Do you offer both vaginal and C-section delivery?你們提供自然分娩及剖腹產嗎？

Yes — both, with a birth-plan conversation in the third trimester. Our specialists deliver at accredited Hong Kong private hospitals and manage the full care pathway: induction, epidural, assisted delivery, elective or emergency caesarean, and immediate postnatal support. See the full comparison below. 是 — 兩種均可提供，並於第三孕期進行分娩計劃諮詢。我們的專科醫生於認可私家醫院接生，並管理整個流程：引產、硬膜外麻醉、助產、擇期或緊急剖腹、及即時產後支援。詳見下方比較表。

FAQ

Common questions常見問題

Short answers to the questions we hear most. For anything specific to your pregnancy, please book a consultation. 以下為最常被問到的問題。如涉及您的個別情況，請預約諮詢。

How many antenatal check-ups will I need?我需要做多少次產前檢查？
A low-risk pregnancy typically involves 10–14 visits — monthly until 28 weeks, fortnightly from 28 to 36 weeks, and weekly thereafter. High-risk pregnancies require more frequent monitoring.低風險孕期通常需要 10–14 次檢查 — 28 週前每月一次、28–36 週每兩週一次、之後每週一次。高危孕婦需要更頻密監測。
Which hospitals do you deliver at?你們在哪些醫院接生？
Our specialists hold admitting privileges at major accredited private hospitals in Hong Kong. The most suitable hospital is discussed during your birth-plan consultation based on your medical needs and preferences.我們的專科醫生於香港多間認可私家醫院擁有接生權。合適的醫院會於分娩計劃諮詢中，按您的醫療需要及個人意願共同決定。
Is NIPT enough, or do I also need an amniocentesis?做了 NIPT 還需要做羊水穿刺嗎？
NIPT is a screening test with >99% detection for common trisomies (21, 18, 13) and is usually sufficient. Amniocentesis is a diagnostic test offered when NIPT is positive, when ultrasound findings warrant it, or when a specific genetic condition is being investigated.NIPT 為篩查測試，對常見三體（21、18、13）檢出率 >99%，一般已足夠。若 NIPT 陽性、超聲有發現或需排除特定遺傳病，則會安排羊水穿刺作診斷。
After a C-section, can I have a vaginal birth next time (VBAC)?曾剖腹，下次可以自然分娩嗎（VBAC）？
Often yes. VBAC has a success rate of 60–80% when the previous caesarean used a low-transverse incision and no new contraindications exist. It requires continuous fetal monitoring and delivery at a facility with immediate theatre access.通常可以。若上次為下段橫切剖腹且無新禁忌，VBAC 成功率為 60–80%。需連續胎心監測並於設有即時手術室的醫院分娩。
When should I start lactation support?甚麼時候開始尋求哺乳支援？
An antenatal lactation session at 34–36 weeks sets expectations and technique. After delivery, the first 72 hours are crucial — our consultants are available in-hospital and for home visits from day one.建議於 34–36 週進行產前哺乳諮詢，預先了解流程及技巧。產後首 72 小時至關重要 — 我們的顧問於醫院及家中均可支援，由第一天開始。
Do you accept medical insurance?你們接受醫療保險嗎？
Yes — we work with major local and international insurers. We can issue pre-authorisation letters and itemised invoices. Please bring your policy details to the first consultation.可以 — 我們與主要本地及國際保險公司合作，可提供預先批核信及詳細收據。請於首次諮詢帶備保單資料。
